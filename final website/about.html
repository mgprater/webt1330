-- about M'S --    

 

My name is Madeline Prater, a 20 yr old born and raised in TN who loves to create meals that are colorful, clean, and satiating :) 

Us type B people don’t really hold value into exact measurements, so for the most part, I measure by “guesstimating” and hoping for the best! So as you look through my instructions, you’ll realize that you can construct beautiful, tasty meals without the stress/worry of making sure every little thing is perfect… this tactic makes being in the kitchen a lot more easy-going and enjoyabl!

So sit back, relax, and enjoy reading through this homey recipes that hold a special place in my heart!
